Bank card payments

Do you work with fiat money?

Yes, we do! Fiat can be used for buying crypto on the exchange. Currently, you can pay with dollars, euros and British pounds, this list may be updated.

I want to buy coins via bank card, what should I do?

You don’t need any special skills and tools to do that.

  1. Choose a fiat currency you will use.
  2. Specify the coin amount you need to buy (there may be limitations per transaction).
  3. Check all entered data to prevent issues.
  4. Enter the address of your cryptocurrency wallet and proceed to purchase a cryptocurrency.
  5. In any case, you will need to confirm your email address by entering the 4-digit verification code that you will receive in your inbox.
  6. Enter your billing and residential address to continue.
  7. Enter bank card data.

Pleases notice for your first coins on the platform you have to pass verification procedure if you hold more than $150. This process is holding taking into account the particularities of your region. You may be asked to show your passport, driving license, etc. Your selfie may be required, this is a standard procedure. We don’t send your data to third parties.

Thus, this procedure may take some time, usually, it’s about 25 minutes or less. After successful verification a transaction will be done, you will get information via e-mail.

Tell me more about double pairs.

It’s simple, we have a fiat proving partner who works with fiat currencies, unfortunately, it can’t operate with double pairs, there are several coins our partner doesn’t operate with.

That’s how our solution works: double pairs go through two platforms instead of one (they go to Moonpay and to our exchange after). So when you need coins our provider doesn’t operate with, your money are exchanged to BTC to be exchanged to the needed coin later. There will be online tracking and e-mail notifications for all operations with double pairs.

Are there any limitations for using cards issued in other regions?

You can use cards issued in any region if you own a card with 3D-secure (this is a protocol developed to improve card security). Although you can use a card with any currency it’s more profitable for you to use debit cards with dollars and euros or pounds. Using a credit card may cause cancellation of your transactions by banks.

What else should I know about fiat payments before using your platform?
